About Us

Built to give artists
more reach, not less.

Xene is a personal artist aggregator. You add the musicians you follow — their SoundCloud, Bandcamp, Twitch, and more — and Xene assembles a single chronological feed of everything they publish, across every platform, in real time.

This is not a streaming service. Xene does not host audio, redistribute content, or create copies of any kind. Every item in the feed is a pointer — a notification that says "your artist just dropped something" — and every click sends the fan directly to the artist's original platform to experience it there.

The problem we are solving is fragmentation. An artist releases music on SoundCloud, posts behind-the-scenes content on Instagram, drops a limited vinyl on Bandcamp, and goes live on Twitch — all in the same week. Their fans miss most of it because no single platform surfaces all of it.

Xene closes that gap. We are a visibility amplifier, not a replacement. Every platform in our ecosystem benefits: more fans discover content, more fans click through, and more fans engage on the platform where the artist actually lives.

What Xene does
  • check_circle Aggregates publicly available artist activity using official platform APIs and public RSS feeds
  • check_circle Sends fans directly to the artist's original platform page on every interaction
  • check_circle Displays platform branding prominently so fans always know where the content originates
  • check_circle Notifies fans when artists go live on Twitch, linking directly to the Twitch stream
  • check_circle Operates within the rate limits, terms of service, and developer guidelines of every platform API it uses
What Xene never does
  • cancel Host, cache, re-stream, or redistribute audio or video content of any kind
  • cancel Replicate or iframe platform experiences in a way that removes traffic from the source
  • cancel Sell, license, or monetize any artist's content — we surface metadata and links only
  • cancel Scrape beyond what is publicly accessible or permitted under each platform's developer terms
  • cancel Allow users to download, save, or own content that belongs to a third-party platform

A note to platform API partners and reviewers

If you are reviewing this application on behalf of SoundCloud, Bandcamp, Twitch, or any other platform — thank you for your time. Xene was built with your platforms' success in mind, not in competition with it.

Our entire value proposition depends on your platform being the destination. If fans stop going to SoundCloud, Xene stops working. We are structurally aligned with your interests. We welcome any questions about our implementation, our API usage patterns, or our roadmap.
